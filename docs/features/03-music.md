# Music

> **Coming Soon** — Music support is planned for a future release. The features described below are under development and not yet available.

Librarr will support music discovery, requests, and library tracking through integration with Lidarr and MusicBrainz.

## Planned Features

### Music Albums

- Album detail pages with cover art, artist, release date, album type (album, single, EP), and genre tags
- Full tracklist display
- Album status and availability tracking

### Artists

- Artist profile pages with biography and discography
- Browse an artist's full album catalog

### Music Requests

- Request albums through the same workflow as books (pending, approved, declined, completed, failed)
- Integration with Lidarr for automated download management
- Download progress tracking from Lidarr queues

### Music Discovery

- Trending music feeds on the home page
- Music search via MusicBrainz

### Metadata

- Album and artist metadata sourced from **MusicBrainz**
- Spotify IDs for cross-referencing
